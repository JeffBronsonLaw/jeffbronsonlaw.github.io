For more than 30 years, Jeffrey I. Bronson, Esq. has been providing clients with legal services for issues like bankruptcy, immigration law, and much more. He is in the practice of law to serve his clients, and his firm takes pride in providing you with a relaxed, caring, and personalized experience. To learn more about Jeff, and how we can assist you. Jeff Bronson is a life-long resident of the State of New Jersey. He resides in Morris County with his wife, Liz. Together they have raised a family and grown a business.

Jeff Bronson is a graduate of Brandeis University and Seton Hall Law School. He has been in general practice for over 30 years and has concentrated on the area of Immigration Law for the last ten years. As a devoted family man and compassionate person, he has found that Immigration Law allows him to practice law while helping to keep families together and offer solutions to people in need. With experience in diverse areas of the law, he is able to handle a wide range of cases, which allows him to become the family attorney to many of his clients and their friends and family.
